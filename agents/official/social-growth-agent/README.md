## What it does

- Creates comprehensive social media strategies with content pillars and KPIs
- Builds weekly/monthly content calendars with captions and hashtag sets
- Designs campaign briefs with creative direction and success metrics
- Provides platform-specific best practices (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube)
- Tracks growth hypotheses and A/B testing plans

## Platforms Supported

Instagram · TikTok · X/Twitter · LinkedIn · YouTube · Facebook · Pinterest · Threads

## Output Formats

| Format | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| Social Media Strategy | Full strategy with pillars, voice, KPIs |
| Content Calendar | Weekly/monthly posting schedule |
| Campaign Brief | Campaign design with creative direction |

## Safety

⚠️ **High-risk agent** — All publishing, scheduling, DM, and ad-buying actions require explicit user approval. This agent drafts content but never auto-publishes.
